About

MindGraph started in 2024 as a part-time endeavour, a place to collect various projects I've worked on in the past and to explore the possibilities of partnering with other companies and individuals who are interested in leveraging data to better understand and act on what's on their mind.

On this page, I describe the mission and vision of Mindsight. If you're interested in the details, you can explore my areas of expertise here.

Mission

My mission is to contribute to a better world by facilitating a deeper understanding of the patterns and phenomena that shape our reality. I am committed to providing decision-makers with practical recommendations based on a solid data foundation. By harnessing my curiosity, dedication, and perseverance, I tackle real-world data challenges, extract meaningful insights, and communicate them in a user-friendly, accessible manner.

Vision

I envision a future where data-driven insights empower decision-makers at all levels, fostering informed choices that lead to a better, more equitable world. To arrive there, I believe we need to reshape the way working with data and analytics is perceived, transforming it from a complex, challenging endeavour into something more relatable and accessible for the average human being. Data-driven insights can help us change the world for the better - provided that we are all on board with the story the data is trying to tell us.
