Services

Throughout my 5+ years of full-time working experience, I've had the opportunity to work on a wide variety of tasks spanning from data collection, transformation and analysis to presenting insights and onboarding users to interactive tools and reports.

What can I do for you?

Some of the areas in which I can help you include:

  • Data extract, transform and load (ETL) project scoping
  • Business Intelligence (BI) & reporting project scoping
  • Analytics/data science project scoping
  • Development of ETL processes (R, Python, Power Query, SQL)
  • Data collection from structured and unstructured sources and data clean-up (R, Python, SQL)
  • Creating, publishing and onboarding users to Power BI reports
  • Creating, publishing and onboarding users to Streamlit data apps (Python)
  • Conducting statistical analyses, including presentation and communication of results (R, Python)
  • Developing statistical/ML predictive models, including presentation and communication of method, result and implications (R, Python)

What data have I worked with?

So far in my career, I've had my hands on a wide variety of data such as:

  • Personal data (HR-related)
  • Educational data
  • Macroeconomic data
  • Political data
  • Pricing data
  • Cross-sectional data
  • Time series data

Common for all of the above is that the data is never perfect. However, it is my conviction that that should never prevent us from using to derive some insights! A proper data clean-up and making some sensible assumptions, combined with a clearly defined question you want to find an answer to go a long way to facilitating a good analytics product.
